DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

The office of the Coordinator, UGC Cell deal with the UGC Section of Dravidian University, Administration Office, where correspondence with the University Grants Commission, Department of Science & Technology, Department of Biotechnology, Indian Council for Medical Research, Department of Environment, Indian Council for Social Sciences etc., New Delhi for sanction and release of grants for implementing various Schemes/ Programmes of UGC and other agencies is made. The activities of the staff are dealing with in detail, are as follows.

  1. Major & Minor Research Projects (Undertaken by the University teachers)
  2. SAP and Non-SAP Programmes undertaken by the Departments in the thrust area identified by U.G.C.
  3. ASIST Programmes undertaken by the Departments where SAP exists.
  4. Implementation of U.G.C. Schemes, like
    • Children Day Care Centre
    • Equal Opportunity Cell
    • Career and Counseling Cell
    • UGC-NET Coaching
    • UGC-Remedial Coaching
    • UGC-Competitive Examinations coaching
    • TEPSE/HEPSN (for disabled persons)
    • Computer Upgradation
    • Central Library
    • Sports and Infrastructure Facilities
    • Basic Facilities for Women
  5. Scholarships
    • Rajiv Gandhi National Fellowship
    • Science Meritorious Fellowship
    • Telugu wordnet and ILCI fellowships
  6. Emeritus Fellowship
  7. Research Awards
  8. Developmental Assistance for Equipment/Books & Journals/Buildings/Campus Development
  9. Unassigned grant for ravel Abroad/Within India to
    • Tattend to International/National Conferences
    • Guest Lectures
    • Organization of Seminars
    • Publication of Learned Research Work/Books
    • Visiting Professorships / Visiting Fellowships
